COVID-19 Vaccination Willingness in California

This narrative visualization explores possible relationships between COVID-19 vaccination willingness and their median household income in California. Vaccination willingness is measured by percentage of people getting at least one dose of any type of vaccine in each county and in each vaccine distribution phase.
