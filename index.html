Does Income Influence Covid-19 Vaccination Willingness?

This narrative visualization explores possible relationships between Covid-19 vaccination willingness and their median household income. Vaccination willingness is measured by percentage of people getting at least one dose of any type of vaccine in all 58 California counties, in Phase 1A, 1B, 1C and 2(current, as of 7/20/2021) of vaccine distribution.
